The Manual beautifully chronicles the maturing of design on the web. It will change how you think about your work.

We hear the voices of a conversation moving through the community of the craftspeople of interactive design: popping up at conferences, in tweets and blog posts, and in adrenalized chats over beer when we gather. The Manual chronicles these voices in a beautiful print artifact. It archives the insights of our best thinkers and writers—both the well known and the quietly working—as we define a more mature understanding of the discipline of web design and more effectively tell the story of our work.
